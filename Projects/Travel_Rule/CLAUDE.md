## Tier Boundaries

When discussing crypto receipts, always respect the tier thresholds: under $1,000 = no action, $1,000–$20,000 = declaration form, over $20,000 = manual Satoshi test by CX team. Do not propose flows that collapse or skip a tier without flagging the compliance impact.
