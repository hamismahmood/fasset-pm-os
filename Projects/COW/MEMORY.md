## Key Themes Emerging (Hamis's calls)

- **USD IBAN + P2P salary flow** is a primary hook for Pakistan-based users receiving USD income
- **Fee sensitivity** is cross-cutting — multiple users cite high fees as the top blocker
- **Dinari dividends gap** blocks serious investor conversion (named explicitly by Muhammad Waleed)
- **App stability** — crashes/freezes flagged unprompted by Zahid Mahmood (6/10 NPS)
- **Card cost saving** lands but isn't driving card adoption — habit/perception barrier, not product
- **Advanced market analytics** missing for power investors (candlestick charts, volume)
- **Due US-based IBAN** launched same day as Muhammad Waleed call — proactive notification gap
