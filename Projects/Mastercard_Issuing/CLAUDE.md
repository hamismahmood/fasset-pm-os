# CLAUDE.md — Mastercard Card Issuing

Project-specific behavioral rules. Cross-cutting rules live in root `CLAUDE.md`. Facts live in `MEMORY.md` (this folder).

## Strategic Framing

This is an open-ended strategic exploration, not a project with a predetermined model. Do NOT assume the answer is a prepaid program manager, a BIN sponsor arrangement, or any other specific structure. The research determines the model. Present options with trade-offs, not conclusions dressed up as options.

## Scope

- Mastercard only. Visa is out of scope for this initiative.
- The question is: what is the right Mastercard relationship for Fasset, if any, and is it worth pursuing over the current multi-issuer model (Rain, Bridge, Reap)?

## PM Ownership

This is a Hamis-led strategic initiative to be proposed to Rafi. Arundathi is the card PM and must be brought in before anything goes to Rafi. Do not draft external comms or formal proposals without flagging that Arundathi alignment comes first.

## Research Standards

- Before stating any regulatory or licensing fact as settled, flag whether it is from a primary source (Mastercard rules doc, regulator circular, Yazan/legal confirmation) or inferred from web research.
- Cross-reference any country coverage or licensing claim against Fasset's actual license footprint: UAE (VARA), Bahrain (CBB Cat 3), Labuan (LFSA), Indonesia (Bappebti).
- Do not cite Rain, Bridge, or Reap coverage as a proxy for what Fasset could achieve directly — that conflates the current model with the proposed one.

## Proposal Gate

The proposal only goes to Rafi after: (1) research is complete, (2) Yazan/legal has confirmed what the licenses permit, (3) Arundathi is aligned.
