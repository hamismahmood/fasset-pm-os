# CLAUDE.md — Global Expansion

Project-specific behavioral rules go here. Cross-cutting rules live in the root `CLAUDE.md`. Project facts live in `MEMORY.md` (this folder).

## Primary Source: Country Prioritization Sheet

When proposing markets, evaluating country feasibility, or referencing prioritization data, **read the `Fasset_Country_Prioritization (WIP)` Google Sheet first** (URL and file ID in `MEMORY.md` under "Working Docs"). It contains the live Rain card analysis, scoring matrix, tier rankings, FX/crypto/P2P metrics, competitor mapping, and recommended actions per market. The summaries in `MEMORY.md` are derived; the sheet is authoritative. If a question depends on data that isn't in `MEMORY.md`, fetch the sheet before answering.

## Country Lists

When proposing or evaluating new markets, always cross-reference: Rain's approved card issuance list (48 countries), Rain's 17 issuance bans and 7 spending bans, Keel's prohibited/restricted jurisdictions, Due's 21 prohibited countries, and Altof's IBAN-disabled list on Confluence. Show the overlap and the gaps before recommending.

## Marketing Constraints

Video ads only (no static), 30 seconds max, must include a human face. In markets where English is 1st or 2nd language, lead with English creative. Do not propose ad concepts that violate these.
