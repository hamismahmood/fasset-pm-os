## 2026-05-19 — Muhammad Waleed

**File:** `muhammad_waleed_call_note.html`
**Interviewer:** Hamis Mahmood
**Source:** Customer Obsession Week (COW)

**Profile:** Fasset consultant employee (tokenization team, B2B partnerships, reports to Rafi). Receives salary in USD IBAN, converts to PKR via P2P. Invests in Shariah-compliant crypto. No card usage. Based in Lahore.

**Key themes:**
- USD IBAN + instant USD→USDT + P2P is his primary salary cashout flow — saves ~15 PKR/$ vs SWIFT + avoids tax
- Dinari products not paying dividends is a named blocker for serious investor conversion
- Advanced market analytics gap vs. dedicated trading platforms
- Knows card saves money on USD spend but hasn't switched — perception/habit barrier
- Due US-based IBAN went live same day; he was unaware — needs a direct nudge

**Product areas:** `#iban` `#p2p` `#investments` `#cards`

**Follow-ups logged:** COW Tracker (May 19 entry). Notify Waleed re: Due US IBAN.

## 2026-05-13 — Waleed Tarek

**File:** `waleed_tarek_call_note.html` (also PDF)
**Interviewer:** Hamis Mahmood
**Source:** Customer Obsession Week (COW)

**Profile:** Pakistani POS business owner. Uses Fasset card for business purchases and P2P transfers. Kirana-style retail business.

**Key themes:**
- Card top-up flow confusing — unclear which payment method is cheapest/fastest
- P2P: wants to send to contacts without knowing their Fasset ID; wants to save frequent recipients
- Notifications absent for received P2P transfers (found out by accident)
- Business use case: would pay a monthly fee for multi-user/employee card access
- Trust signals matter: would refer if he could show friends official Fasset branding

**Product areas:** `#cards` `#p2p` `#notifications` `#business`

**Follow-ups logged:** COW Tracker row 4 (May 13 entry).
