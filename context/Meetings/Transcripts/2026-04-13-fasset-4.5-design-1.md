## Topics covered (architecture)
- **Unified cash account** replacing all IBANs — fully spendable through the card.
- "Total Balance" → **"Total Net Worth"** (reduce confusion between cash and investment values).
- Balance breakdown under info icon (percentage breakdown removed from home screen).
- **Verification Center** for document uploads.
- **Activity Center** to surface mandatory user actions.
- Horizontal scrolling cards (rejected gold premium aesthetic).
- React Native confirmed as front-end mobile stack.

## Money movement features
- Distinct "Request" and "Send" CTAs.
- Money movement via Fasset tag, phone number, or payment link.
- New Payments section for managing incoming requests.
- Send limited to currently held assets/currencies.
